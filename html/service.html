Production

We’ve invested in state of the art facilities to offer turnkey production solutions covering digital, video and audio creation. We craft original content in-house through an accomplished team of producers, directors, animators and engineers to delight and inspire. From compelling live action production to fully animated videos relying on 2D or 3D graphics, OnBoard delivers compelling visual content crafted to client needs. We have an established track record of producing safety videos, welcome films, destination documentaries and advertisements. Our offer full spectrum capabilities – from scripting and shooting to post-production and subtitling – offer our partners a turnkey journey from start to finish. Effective, powerful and targeted, our video productions bring original ideas to breathtaking life.
